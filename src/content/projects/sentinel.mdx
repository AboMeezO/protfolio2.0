## Vision and Paradigm

Sentinel is a comprehensive financial orchestration layer designed to transform Discord into a professional storefront. It provides the robust infrastructure required to handle high-stakes financial transactions with the precision and reliability of a dedicated banking interface.

## Architectural Excellence

The system is built upon a distributed monorepo architecture, ensuring maximum reliability and scale.

### Core Pillars:
- **NestJS API**: The authoritative source of truth, managing complex PayPal orchestrations and secure credential vaulting.
- **Discord Intelligence**: A sophisticated interaction engine providing a seamless, interactive UI for financial management.
- **Security Foundation**: Implementing industry-leading cryptographic standards like **AES-256-GCM** to protect sensitive merchant data.

## Enterprise-Grade Features

Sentinel goes beyond simple payments. It offers a suite of tools for professional financial management, including real-time balance auditing, automated invoice lifecycle tracking, and a multi-tenant infrastructure that supports isolated shops across different servers.

## Operational Integrity

By centralizing invoice management and real-time transaction tracking within Discord, Sentinel empowers organizations to scale their operations without compromising on professional standards. It is the definitive choice for communities that demand the highest standards of security and functionality.
