## Project Overview

EV King V2 was engineered to elevate the digital experience of electric vehicle purchasing. By moving away from a restrictive WordPress monolith, we built a fully decoupled, headless architecture that provides absolute control over the user journey, data relationships, and business logic.

## Technical Innovation

The platform is a masterclass in modern web performance. We leveraged **React 19** and **Vite** to ensure instantaneous navigation, while the backend is powered by a custom **NestJS** infrastructure that acts as a headless CMS.

### Key Highlights:

- **Type-Safe Inventory**: Using **Prisma** to map complex vehicle specs directly from the database to the UI, ensuring data integrity across the platform.
- **Performance Orchestration**: A hybrid animation approach using **GSAP** for complex scroll interactions and **Framer Motion** for layout transitions, all optimized to run at 60fps on mobile.
- **Identity & Security**: Offloaded identity management to **Clerk** while maintaining relational mappings for user favorites and leads via secure webhooks.

## Beyond Simple Browsing

This isn't just a catalog; it's a tool for decision-making. We integrated bespoke financial calculators for invoice estimation and EV fuel savings, transforming the site into a high-engagement platform that assists buyers throughout their purchasing journey.
